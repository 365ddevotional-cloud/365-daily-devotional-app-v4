We are adding a NEW FEATURE to the 365 Daily Devotional App.

FEATURE NAME:
Daily Promises of God (In-App Notifications)

Goal:
Deliver TWO daily inspirational Bible promise notifications to users inside the app using the existing in-app messaging system.

Source Content:
Use the "500 God's Promises for Believers" dataset.

Example entries include:

God promises salvation to those who believe in Jesus — John 3:16
God promises peace that surpasses understanding — Philippians 4:7
God promises strength when you are weak — Isaiah 40:29
God promises to guide your path — Proverbs 3:5–6
God promises that He works all things for good — Romans 8:28
God promises that nothing can separate us from His love — Romans 8:38–39

These promises must be used as Bible quotes.

----------------------------------------------------

FEATURE REQUIREMENTS

1. TWO DAILY PROMISE NOTIFICATIONS

Each user receives:

Promise #1 in the morning
Promise #2 nine hours later

Example schedule:

First notification: 8:00 AM
Second notification: 5:00 PM

Use a scheduler or cron job to trigger the messages daily.

----------------------------------------------------

2. MESSAGE FORMAT

Each notification must include:

• Catchy heading
• Bible promise text
• Bible reference
• “From the 365DailyDevotional” stamp

Example:

Heading:
God Is Working Everything For Your Good

Card Message:
"And we know that God causes everything to work together for the good of those who love God."
Romans 8:28

Stamp:
From the 365DailyDevotional

----------------------------------------------------

3. BEAUTIFUL 3D PROMISE CARD

Each promise must appear in a BEAUTIFUL 3D CARD.

Card design requirements:

• Soft glowing background
• Gold or light rays
• Rounded corners
• Subtle 3D shadow
• Gradient background
• Different colors every time
• Light animation glow

Each card must be visually DIFFERENT from the previous one.

Examples of color themes:

Gold + White
Purple + Light
Blue + Heaven glow
Sunrise gradient
Soft green peace theme

----------------------------------------------------

4. RANDOM PROMISE ENGINE

Create a Promise Engine that:

• pulls from the 500 promise dataset
• ensures promises rotate sequentially
• prevents repeats until all 500 are used
• stores last delivered promise index

Storage can be:

database table
or
JSON store

Example structure:

promises_table

id
promise_text
scripture_reference
heading

delivery_state

last_index
last_sent_time

----------------------------------------------------

5. NOTIFICATION TYPES

Send the notification through:

1) In-app message popup
2) Push notification
3) Notification center inside the app

Popup example:

"God's Promise for You Today"

Then show the 3D promise card.
